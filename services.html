What We Offer

Services

Residential

For Your Home

01

Full-Service Interior Design

Our most comprehensive offering. We handle everything — space planning, concept development, material and finish selection, furniture procurement, contractor coordination, and final installation. You walk in on reveal day to a fully realized space.

  • Initial discovery & site measure
  • Space planning & layout
  • Concept boards & material palette
  • 3D renderings
  • Furniture & fixture procurement
  • Contractor coordination
  • Final styling & installation
02

Interior Styling

For spaces that are structurally complete but need the finishing layer — art, accessories, textiles, plants, and the thoughtful arrangement that makes a room feel curated rather than assembled.

  • In-home consultation
  • Art & accessory sourcing
  • Textile layering
  • Day-of styling
03

Design Consultation

A focused two-hour session with Alex — ideal for clients who want expert direction and a clear action plan they can execute themselves. Perfect for a single room, a renovation decision, or a color and finish question.

  • Two-hour in-home session
  • Prioritized action plan
  • Product & vendor recommendations
  • Follow-up summary notes
04

3D Renderings

Photorealistic visualizations that allow you to see and approve a design before committing to any purchases or construction. Available as a standalone service or as part of a full-service engagement.

  • Measured floor plan input
  • Furniture & finish modeling
  • Two rounds of revisions
  • High-resolution deliverables
