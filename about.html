The Designer

Alexandra Hurwitz

About

Design rooted in
how you live

Alex founded Interiors x Alex in 2024 on a simple belief: that great design isn't about following trends or impressing guests. It's about creating spaces that feel authentically, deeply yours.

With nearly 10 years of experience across New Jersey and New York, Alex brings a refined aesthetic sensibility and a sharp eye for detail to every engagement. Whether she's sourcing a one-of-a-kind vintage piece or guiding a client through a full renovation, the guiding question is always the same: does this serve the person who will live here?

The studio works with a carefully curated roster of contractors, artisans, and vendors. Relationships built over years to ensure that every project is executed with the same care that goes into its design.

The Approach

Design Philosophy

01

Listen First

Every project begins with a deep discovery conversation. We want to understand not just your aesthetic preferences, but how you move through your home and what you need it to feel like.

02

Design with Purpose

Beauty without function is decoration. Every decision, from the furniture plan to the light switch placement, is made with both form and daily life in mind.

03

Source Thoughtfully

We balance investment pieces with accessible finds, always prioritizing quality materials, longevity, and the kind of character that only improves with time.

04

Execute Flawlessly

The install day is the payoff, and we ensure it goes smoothly. Our network of trusted contractors and vendors means your project lands exactly as designed.
