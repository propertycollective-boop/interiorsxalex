Interiors x Alex ("Studio," "we," "us," or "our") is a luxury interior design studio based in New Jersey. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information submitted through interiorsxalex.com (the "Site"). By using the Site, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly when you submit our contact form, including:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Project location, type, and timeline
  • Any details you share in your message

We use the following third-party tracking tools:

  • Google Analytics 4 — tracks page visits, traffic sources, and general usage patterns to help us understand how visitors interact with the site. Data is anonymized and processed by Google. Google Privacy Policy.

We do not sell your personal data to any third party.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through the contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry and communicate about potential or active design projects. We do not add you to any marketing list without your explicit consent.

3. How We Store and Protect Your Information

Inquiry submissions are transmitted via encrypted email (TLS) and stored only as long as necessary to fulfill your request or maintain our business relationship. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except as necessary to operate the Site (such as our web hosting provider) or as required by law.

5. Your Rights Under New Jersey Law

If you are a New Jersey resident, you may have rights under the New Jersey Data Privacy Act (NJDPA), including the right to:

  • Know what personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal data (we do not sell or share data)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@interiorsxalex.com. We will respond within 45 days as required by law.

6. Children's Privacy

This Site is not directed at individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has submitted information to us, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.
