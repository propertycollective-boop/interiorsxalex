Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 11, 2025 | Last Updated: July 11, 2025

Interiors x Alex ("Studio," "we," "us," or "our") is a luxury interior design studio based in Colts Neck, New Jersey. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect personal information submitted through interiorsxalex.com (the "Site"). By using the Site, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Information We Collect

We collect two types of information:

Information you provide directly when you submit our contact form:

  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number (optional)
  • Project location, type, and timeline
  • Any details you share in your message

Information collected automatically when you visit the Site, including your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the Site. This is collected via cookies and third-party tracking tools described in Section 3 below.

2. How We Use Your Information

Information submitted through the contact form is used solely to respond to your inquiry and communicate about potential or active design projects. We do not add you to any marketing list without your explicit consent.

Automatically collected information is used to understand how visitors use the Site, measure the effectiveness of our content, and deliver relevant advertising on third-party platforms.

3. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

This Site uses cookies and similar tracking technologies. We use the following third-party tools:

  • Google Analytics 4 — collects anonymized data about page visits, traffic sources, and general usage patterns. Data is processed by Google LLC. You can opt out via Google Analytics Opt-out or Google Privacy Policy.
  • Meta Pixel (Facebook/Instagram) — tracks page visits and user actions to enable ad retargeting on Facebook and Instagram. Data is processed by Meta Platforms, Inc. You can manage your ad preferences at Meta Privacy Policy or via Meta Ad Preferences.

By continuing to use this Site, you consent to the use of these cookies. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features of the Site may not function as intended.

4. How We Store and Protect Your Information

Inquiry submissions are transmitted via encrypted email (TLS) and stored only as long as necessary to fulfill your request or maintain our business relationship. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

5. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • As necessary to operate the Site (web hosting provider, Render.com)
  • With Google and Meta as described in Section 3 above
  • As required by applicable law or legal process

6. Your Rights Under New Jersey Law

If you are a New Jersey resident, you may have rights under the New Jersey Data Privacy Act (NJDPA), including the right to:

  • Know what personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction or deletion of your personal data
  • Opt out of the sale or sharing of your personal data (we do not sell data)
  • Opt out of targeted advertising (you may do so via Meta Ad Preferences or Google Analytics Opt-out)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at hello@interiorsxalex.com. We will respond within 45 days as required by law.

7. Children's Privacy

This Site is not directed at individuals under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If you believe a minor has submitted information to us, please contact us and we will promptly delete it.

8. Third-Party Links

The Site may contain links to third-party platforms such as Instagram. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those platforms and encourage you to review their policies separately.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date. Continued use of the Site after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
